My Transformation Story

I used to feel invisible. People would laugh at me because of my weight and every comment made my confidence drop. I hated how I looked, and I avoided mirrors. But in December 2024, I decided it was time to take control. I wanted to prove to myself that I could change. It is not for anyone else, but for me.
