My Experience

Final Year Project – Community Engagement

As part of my final year project, I was involved in the Program Sentuhan Komuniti Bestari 2025 held at Perpustakaan Desa Taman Intan Perdana, Port Dickson. The program focused on community engagement and knowledge sharing. As the Programme & Protocol Bureau, my responsibilities included:

  • Ensuring smooth program flow and protocol
  • Handling a checkpoint for the Trail of Knowledge activity
  • Setting up booths for the exhibition
  • Assisting with overall event management

This project provided me with valuable experience in organizational skills, teamwork, and protocol management during formal community events.

Internship – Pejabat Daerah dan Tanah Klang

During my internship, I was assigned to the ICT Unit, where I gained hands-on experience in supporting daily operations and technical services. My responsibilities included:

  • Digitizing physical records into digital formats
  • Preparing ICT equipment for meetings and programs
  • Designing and installing informational posters
  • Assisting with live streaming on Facebook

This internship provided me with valuable exposure to ICT-related workflows and highlighted the importance of accuracy and efficiency in administrative support.

Merdeka Night Parade – Selangor State

I proudly represented Pejabat Daerah dan Tanah Klang in the Selangor State Merdeka Night Parade, which was organised at the state level in conjunction with Malaysia’s Independence Day celebration. As part of the contingent, I participated in ceremonial formations together with representatives from various government departments. Prior to the event, several training and rehearsal sessions were conducted to ensure proper coordination, discipline, and uniformity among all participants.

Through this experience, I learned the importance of teamwork, time management, and following instructions, as each participant played an important role in ensuring the success of the parade. Participating in this event also gave me valuable exposure to formal state functions and strengthened my sense of responsibility and pride in representing my internship department at a high-profile event.
